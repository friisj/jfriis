# Pulse - Initial Research

> Landscape survey and foundational research for the project.

---

## Problem Space

Institutional knowledge decays rapidly. New hires forget onboarding content within weeks. Strategy shifts announced in all-hands or Slack channels are absorbed by a fraction of the team. Process documentation exists but nobody re-reads it. The result: misalignment, repeated questions, and a widening gap between what the organization knows collectively and what any individual retains.

Current solutions (LMS platforms, wikis, onboarding checklists) are passive — they deliver information once and hope it sticks. Active reinforcement of workplace-specific knowledge barely exists outside of compliance training.

## Prior Art

| Approach | Strengths | Weaknesses | Relevance |
|----------|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Anki / spaced repetition apps | Proven learning science, individual control | No corporate context integration, manual card creation | Core mechanic to adopt |
| LMS platforms (Lessonly, Docebo) | Enterprise-grade, compliance tracking | Heavy, course-oriented, not contextual | Competitive reference |
| Slack bots (Triviabot, etc.) | Lightweight, in-channel delivery | Generic trivia, no corporate context | Delivery channel inspiration |
| Guru / Slite knowledge bases | Connected to workflows, searchable | Passive — no active recall mechanism | Context source, not competitor |
| Quizlet for teams | Card-based, team sharing | Manual content creation, no tool integration | UX reference |

## Key Questions

1. What's the minimum viable integration surface? (Just Slack? Slack + Notion?)
2. How good is LLM-generated question quality from real corporate content?
3. What quiz formats work best for workplace context? (MCQ, true/false, fill-in, scenario-based?)
4. How do you handle sensitive/confidential content in quiz generation?
5. What's the right cadence — daily micro-quizzes or weekly batches?
6. How do you avoid "quiz fatigue" in a workplace setting?
7. What's the B2B distribution model — Slack app marketplace? Direct sales?
